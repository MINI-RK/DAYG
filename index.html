3:Amazon FBA

Amazon FBA is a service that allows sellers to store their products in Amazon's fulfillment centers and have Amazon handle the picking, packing, shipping, and customer service for those products. This can be a great way for sellers to save time and money, and to offer their customers a more convenient shopping experience.

4:youtube automation

YouTube automation is the use of tools and services to automate tasks on YouTube, such as uploading videos, scheduling videos, and managing comments. This can be a great way to save time and grow your channel more efficiently.

5:Ghost ecommerce

Ghost ecommerce is a way to add an online store to your Ghost blog. It allows you to sell products directly from your blog, without having to use a separate ecommerce platform. Ghost ecommerce is powered by Snipcart, a headless commerce platform that makes it easy to add ecommerce functionality to any website.
